About Us

Rachnatmak Infracon LLP

Rachnatmak Infracon LLP (RIL) is a major manufacturing, construction and production services conglomerate with local and global operations. With 10 years of a strong, customer focused approach and a continuous quest for world-class quality, we have unmatched expertise across Building Construction & Infrastructure Projects and maintain a leadership in all our major lines of business.

Every aspect of RIL businesses is characterised by professionalism and high standards of corporate governance. Sustainability is embedded into our long-term strategy for growth.
